# 📉 Tech Layoffs 2026 Data Insights Dashboard

An interactive, high-impact Tableau dashboard built to analyze and visualize the tech industry layoffs of 2026. This project focuses on identifying key trends, tracking affected sectors, measuring the correlation with AI investments, and presenting financial impacts in a clean, executive-ready format.

## 🚀 Key Insights & Features

* *Executive KPIs:* Real-time tracking of *Total Jobs Cut, **Average % Workforce Impacted, and **Simultaneous AI Investment ($B)*.
* *The 2026 AI Trend:* A deep-dive analysis into whether layoffs were directly driven by artificial intelligence integration (*AI Cited* analysis).
* *Company & Sector Analysis:* Top-performing and most-affected companies sorted by severity, color-coded by industry sector.
* *Layoff Timeline:* A monthly trend analysis showing how the layoffs peaked or stabilized throughout 2026.
* *Global Filters:* Fully interactive dashboard where clicking on specific sectors or AI metrics instantly updates the entire view.
* 
## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Skills Demonstrated

* *Tool:* Tableau Desktop
* *Data Cleaning & Feature Engineering:* Structured and reduced a messy dataset by filtering redundant fields, fixing decimal percentage glitches via advanced formatting, and preparing high-cardinality text for visualization.
* *Dashboard Design:* Utilized customized floating layouts for pixel-perfect visualization, clean UI contrast, and optimized scannability.
  
## 📂 Dataset Architecture (Cleaned Fields)

The project involved transforming raw data by removing administrative/useless text and focusing on core fields:
* Jobs Cut & Pct Workforce Cut (Primary Metrics)
* AI Cited & Simultaneous AI Investment bn (Advanced Trends)
* Company, Sector, Country, Region (Dimensions)
* Layoff Data (Time Series)
* Company Revenue 2025 bn & Stock Reaction (Financials)
